Our Story

Parents-at-heart
on a mission

We're not a faceless startup. We're four people who got frustrated with the same problem every Australian parent knows too well — and decided to actually fix it.

Why we built Kidsplor
"There are thousands of brilliant activity providers across Australia. Parents have no good way to find them."

Swim schools buried on page four of Google. Dance studios discoverable only by someone's mum on Facebook. Coding camps that fill up before the word even spreads. We kept hearing the same story from parents everywhere: too many tabs open, too many WhatsApp threads, and still ending up with the same three results. Meanwhile, kids deserve to explore the things they love — beyond whatever the algorithm decides to show that day. Kidsplor exists to change that. A calendar that runs itself. Activities that find you. A family life that actually feels manageable.
