Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the personal information that we hold, and to collecting, using and disclosing it in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including the Australian Privacy Principles in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (Privacy Act).

'We', 'us' or 'our' means Kidsplor Pty Ltd (Kidsplor). We respect the confidentiality of the personal information held by Kidsplor and take steps to protect the privacy of Kidsplor users and their families as outlined below. We recognise the sensitivity of personal information — particularly in relation to families and children — and have specifically limited the information we gather, making as much as possible optional.

We will only use or disclose personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy, or where we have your consent. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information we hold, including how we collect, use and disclose it.

The Personal Information We Collect (and Why)

To provide you with the Web App Services and for other purposes as described in this Privacy Policy, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account registration: When you register an Account, we collect limited personal information including your name, email address, and any photos you choose to upload. The name you select does not need to include your real name or any information that identifies you.
  • Optional family details: You may optionally provide details about your children (such as first names and approximate ages) to improve activity filtering and recommendations. These details do not need to be exact or identifiable.
  • User-generated content: We collect personal information you choose to post within the App, including reviews and photos. Please be aware that reviews and photos posted within the App will be visible to others. We recommend you do not post sensitive or private information about yourself or others.
  • Payment information: All payments are processed via your payment service provider (such as the app store or a third-party payment processor). We maintain access to transactional account information only via these services and do not have access to your credit card details.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may collect information relating to your Web App activity to help us improve the experience and customise content. This includes functional cookies (which remember your preferences) and strictly necessary cookies (required for the Web App to operate). We may also use resettable device identifiers on mobile devices for analytics, feed personalisation, and push notifications. You can opt out of some push notification types from within the Web App.

Location data

With your permission, we may collect your device's physical location to provide nearby activity recommendations. You can withdraw this consent at any time via your device settings, though doing so will limit the Web App's core discovery functionality.

Why We Use and/or Disclose the Personal Information We Hold

To provide you with Web App Services

We use your email address to register and manage your Account. We may contact you, or ask a Service Provider to contact you on our behalf, about your Account or to assist in improving the Web App Services. We use device data (with your permission) to provide location-relevant activity suggestions.

For marketing analytics

We may use de-identified, aggregated data — including location data — to better understand user preferences and improve our products. We may provide this aggregated, de-identified data to third parties to assist them in understanding general trends. We do not sell personal data to third parties for advertising purposes.

To meet our legal obligations

  • Complying with applicable laws, including the Australian Consumer Law and the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth);
  • Fulfilling our obligations under contracts with you or third parties;
  • Protecting our rights or the rights of others, including for fraud or risk management purposes; and
  • Complying with requests from government authorities and/or enforcement bodies.

When engaging with Service Providers

We use third-party suppliers (Service Providers) to support our business, including IT services, servers, payment processing, and marketing analytics. These Service Providers may access your personal information to the extent required to provide their services, and we only disclose personal information to them in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable privacy laws.

For marketing

We may use personal information to contact you about offers or promotions relevant to you, where we have a lawful basis to do so. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Social media

If you interact with us via a social media account, your information will also be subject to the privacy policy of that social media platform. We may use your data to display targeted ads about our products on social media platforms you are registered with.

Reviews

Reviews you post via the Web App may be displayed within the Web App and may be shared with our Business Partners, Service Providers, and related entities for promotional purposes.

Storage and Security of Personal Information

We take all reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our security measures include:

  • Storing personal information on secure networks consistent with industry standards;
  • Using encryption when transferring personal information between servers;
  • Restricting access to personal information to employees, contractors, and agents who need it to process it for us; and
  • Authenticating Web App users before providing access to their Accounts.

How Long Do We Keep Your Personal Information?

If we no longer need personal information we hold about you, we take steps to delete, destroy, or de-identify it. We may retain personal information for insurance and/or audit purposes, or to comply with legal obligations including under the Australian Consumer Law and the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth).

Account deletion: To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, please contact us at hello@kidsplor.com.au. We will process your request in accordance with this Privacy Policy and our obligations under the Privacy Act.

Other Data Protection Rights

In some circumstances, depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right to object: You can object at any time to your personal data being processed for direct marketing purposes, or for targeted advertising on social media.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Right to restriction: You can request restriction of the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request portability of your personal data where processing is based on consent or the performance of a contract.
  • Right to erasure: You can request erasure of your personal data where it is no longer necessary for the purpose of the data processing.

Contact Us

You can:

  • Request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request to opt out of or unsubscribe from communications from us; or
  • Make a privacy complaint or inquiry — including regarding applicable data protection rights — by contacting us at hello@kidsplor.com.au.

How to make a privacy complaint

If you have concerns about how we have handled your personal information and wish to make a privacy complaint, please contact us using the details above. If we are unable to resolve your complaint, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at: GPO Box 5218, Sydney NSW 2001, www.oaic.gov.au (telephone 1300 363 992).
