Flexible Features for your business

Everything you need to run and grow your classes

Built by people who've talked to hundreds of instructors, studios and small businesses. Every feature solves a real problem.

🔍

Smart Listings

Your classes appear in front of the right families at the right time — filtered by age, suburb, schedule and category. No boosted posts, no algorithms to fight. Just organic discovery.

Be found, not buried
📅

Class Schedule Manager

Create recurring classes, set capacity limits, add coaches, and manage timetables visually. Parents see live availability; you see who's coming and when — all in one calendar view.

Replace your spreadsheet
👥

Enrolments & Waitlists

Parents enrol in seconds. When a class fills up, they join a waitlist automatically and get notified the moment a spot opens. You're always full — without lifting a finger.

Zero empty spots
💳

Payments & Auto-Renewals

Secure payments via Stripe — terms, casual classes, or custom plans. Auto-renewals mean your cash flow is predictable. No more chasing down bank transfers or handling cash at the door.

Get paid on time, always
💬

Parent Inbox

Message parents directly from your dashboard. Send class reminders, cancellation notices or term updates in one click. No more Facebook messages, WhatsApp groups or emails going to spam.

One inbox for everything
📊

Revenue & Analytics

See term-on-term growth, enrolment trends, class fill rates and projected revenue at a glance. Works for solo instructors and multi-coach studios alike. Know your numbers without an accountant.
